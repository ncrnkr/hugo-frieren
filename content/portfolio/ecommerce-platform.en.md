The mage exam – a competition that brings together Frieren's past and present.

## The Exam

To enter the holy territories, Frieren needs the rank of First-Class Mage. The exam brings together the strongest mages on the continent.

## The Challenges

- Passing the preliminary exam in groups of three
- Navigating complex labyrinths
- Direct confrontation with examiners
- Demonstrating both strength and resourcefulness

## Encounters

During the exam, Frieren meets other fascinating mages – each with their own story and motivation. The exam shows that strength alone isn't enough – cooperation and understanding are equally important.
