Frieren's second great journey – this time with Fern and Stark, seeking the place where she can speak with Himmel.

## The Goal

The city of Ende lies beyond the northern edge of the continent. There, it is said to be possible to speak with the souls of the departed. Frieren wants to tell Himmel that she finally understands him.

## The Companions

- Fern as magical apprentice and moral compass
- Stark as protector and lovable coward
- Encounters with old acquaintances and new allies

## Realizations

On this journey, Frieren learns to live in the moment – something Himmel always wanted to teach her.
